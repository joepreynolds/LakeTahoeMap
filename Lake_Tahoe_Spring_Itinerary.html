🚤 Friday, May 30 – Lake Day & Lakeside Dining

Morning

  • Quick snack at the Airbnb

10:00 AM – Grocery Store Run

  • Stock up on snacks, drinks, sunscreen, and other essentials for the boat day
  • Good spots: Raley’s, Safeway, or Whole Foods
⸻ ⸻ ⸻ ⸻

1:30 PM – 6:00 PM – 4-Hour Boat Adventure with Tahoe Sports
📍 Tahoe Sports, 900 Ski Run Blvd, South Lake Tahoe, CA 96150.

🌊 Full Boating Itinerary

  • 🚤 Check-in and safety briefing, load up your gear
  • 🌊 Cruise to Emerald Bay: mountain & lake views
  • 🏝️ Explore Emerald Bay: circle Fannette Island, Vikingsholm Castle
  • 🚤 Cruise to Kiva Beach: gentle lake breeze
  • 🏖️ Relax at Kiva Beach: swim, float, toast to the day
  • 🚤 Short cruise to Baldwin Beach: stunning Sierra backdrop
  • 🏖️ Explore Baldwin Beach: stroll hand-in-hand, snap photos
  • 🚤 Return to the marina: watch the sunset colors
⸻ ⸻ ⸻ ⸻

7:00 PM – 8:30 PM – Dinner at Riva Grill
📍 900 Ski Run Blvd, South Lake Tahoe, CA 96150

  • Casual lakeside dining with a sunset view
  • Fresh seafood, delicious cocktails, and a cozy vibe
⸻ ⸻ ⸻ ⸻

8:30 PM onwards – Back to the Airbnb

  • Hot tub soak, music, drinks, and winding down the day
