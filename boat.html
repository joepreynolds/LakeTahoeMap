Lake Tahoe Spring Getaway: Boat Day Itinerary

🌊 Full Itinerary

🚤 Friday, May 30 – 4-Hour Boat Adventure with Tahoe Sports

📍 Tahoe Sports, 900 Ski Run Blvd, South Lake Tahoe, CA 96150

1:30 PM – 2:00 PM – Check-in and Safety Briefing

  • Meet the staff at Tahoe Sports for paperwork and a safety overview.
  • Load up your gear and get comfortable on your boat.

🌊 2:00 PM – 2:45 PM – Cruise to Emerald Bay

  • Enjoy spectacular mountain and lake views as you head west.
  • Keep an eye out for bald eagles and osprey!
  • Snap a few photos of the boat and the shimmering water.

🏝️ 2:45 PM – 3:15 PM – Explore Emerald Bay

  • Circle Fannette Island and admire the granite cliffs and green pines.
  • Take in the views of the historic Vikingsholm Castle from the water.
  • Pause to just soak in the beauty—it’s one of Tahoe’s most iconic spots.

🚤 3:15 PM – 3:45 PM – Cruise to Kiva Beach

  • Follow the southern shore of Lake Tahoe eastward.
  • Enjoy the gentle lake breeze and crystal-clear water.

🏖️ 3:45 PM – 4:45 PM – Relax at Kiva Beach

  • Anchor your boat and hop in for a swim or a float.
  • Unpack a light picnic or snacks, and toast to your day on the lake.
  • Take a moment to just chill—this is what lake days are all about!

🚤 4:45 PM – 5:00 PM – Short Cruise to Baldwin Beach

  • Baldwin Beach is a quick ride from Kiva.
  • Enjoy the stunning Sierra Nevada backdrop along the shoreline.

🏖️ 5:00 PM – 5:30 PM – Explore Baldwin Beach

  • Step ashore and walk the sandy beach hand-in-hand.
  • Snap some final photos in the late afternoon light—sun-kissed memories!

🚤 5:30 PM – 6:00 PM – Return to the Marina

  • Enjoy a smooth, relaxing cruise back to Ski Run Marina.
  • Watch the changing colors of the sky as the sun starts its slow descent.
